FAQ

Common questions answered
What is the difference between GI and GP pipes?+
Both are galvanized steel pipes, but differ in how the zinc coating is applied:
GI Pipes (Galvanized Iron):
  • Coated via hot-dip galvanization ‚Äî submerged in molten zinc for thick, robust coating
  • Superior corrosion resistance ‚Äî ideal for harsh environments, water supply, structural support
  • Heavier and more durable ‚Äî oil & gas, bridges, heavy-duty applications
GP Pipes (Galvanized Pipes):
  • Pre-galvanized steel ‚Äî thinner zinc coating applied before forming
  • Lighter and easier to handle ‚Äî good for fencing, scaffolding, irrigation
  • More cost-effective for general-purpose applications
